If he ever grows up, he wants to be a pirate but, in the meantime, works as a code–monkey and visionary or naysayer, as the need arises, for [Yahoo!][] Europe. Prior to this, Steve studied at the [University of Surrey][UniS] (in Guildford), graduating with a Bachelor of Science degree (with first class honours, shockingly) in [Computer Science & Engineering][CSE], and worked for [Fujitsu Siemens Computers][FSC]. Steve adores music of almost any form, and can often be found making excuses to buy *just one more cd*. His music collection is somewhere around the 500-album mark and rising; if you ask nicely, he might suggest something for you to listen to. Steve also likes film and dead-tree-based entertainment in equal measure, with something of a penchant for the [works][discworld] of Terry Pratchett. He enjoys video games, but believes that they just don't make 'em as good as they [used to][monkey island]. Steve can usually be found (when not at work) in front of one of his [two shiny new Macs][mac photo] tinkering with html, css, or something else thats equally geeky. ##Contact## If you feel the need to contact me, mail me at journal (at) nascentguruism dot com.
